Why buy land in Lapland & Northern Finland

Northern Finland offers large natural areas, forests, rivers and lakes, with low population density and clean natural environments.

Land ownership in Finland is secure and transparent, and property transactions can be completed electronically through the Finnish Land Information System.

Land areas across Lapland & Northern Finland

Available land areas are located across Finnish Lapland and Northern Finland. Browse properties by municipality using the map and list above.

Many areas are located near forests, rivers and lakes in quiet northern wilderness landscapes.
